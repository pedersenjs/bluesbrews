Blue's Brews is a bottle shop located in Boone, North Carolina, that features local beers, seltzers, kombucha, and more from over 60 different local breweries!

Blue's Brews officially opened in 2022 and is located at 362 W King St, Boone, NC 28607.

Contact Blue's Brews at (828) 278-9243 with any questions or requests!
